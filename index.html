Enchiladas

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Tortillas 6
Enchilada sauce 16 oz
Shredded cheese 8 oz
Shredded chicken 12 oz

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350ºF.

Step 2: In a large bowl, combine cooked shredded chicken with 1/4 cup enchilada sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Warm the tortillas in a microwave for 1 minute, flipping the them halfway through until they’re warm and pliable.

Step 4: Assemble the enchiladas by filling each tortilla evenly with the shredded chicken mixture and a generous pinch of shredded cheese. Roll them tightly to close and place in a large baking dish seam side down.

Step 5: Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the tortillas and then top them with the remaining cheese.

Step 6: Bake for 20 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve.

Tacos

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
90% to 93% lean ground beef 1 lb
Chili powder 1 tablespoon
Ground cumin 1 teaspoon
Salt 3/4 teaspoon
Dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon
Garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon
Ground black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Taco shells 12

Step 1: Add the beef to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Break the meat apart with a wooden spoon. Add the chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and pepper to the meat. Stir well. Cook until the meat is cooked through, about 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: Reduce the heat to medium. Add the tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 7-8 minutes, until some of the liquid evaporates but the meat mixture is still a little saucy. Remove from the heat.

Step 3: Warm the taco shells according to their package directions.

Step 4: Fill the taco shells with 2 heaping tablespoons of taco meat. Top with desired taco toppings: shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, diced red onion, taco sauce, sour cream, guacamole, etc.

Guacamole

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 16 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe avocados 2
Small onion 1
Ripe tomato 1
Garlic clove 1
Lime 1
Salt and pepper A pinch

Step 1: Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.

Step 2: Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
